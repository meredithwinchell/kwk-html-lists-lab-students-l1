Ultimate Grilled Cheese

  1. Heat frying pan at medium heat and add butter
  2. Place bread slices over and add slices of American or cheddar cheese to one
  3. Top cheese with shredded parmesan or pepper jack cheese
  4. Cover cheese with second slice of bread and fry over 1 minute
  5. Flip grilled cheese over and fry for 2 minutes
